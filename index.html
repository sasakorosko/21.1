Website of the esports organization NAVI PUBG

Navi (Natus Vincere) is a Ukrainian esports organization that was established in 2009, and to this day, it continues to develop and find talents that amaze the world stage. Navi PUBG is one of the organization's rosters, an esports team that represents Ukraine on the world arena of PUBG esports. It popularizes this discipline and encourages young players to develop their skills.
